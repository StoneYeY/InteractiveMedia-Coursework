DTFT (discrete time Fourier transform) enables us to analyze the spectrum of discrete-time signals and the frequency response characteristics of discrete-time systems in the frequency domain (digital frequency domain). But there are two practical problems.

The connection of FFT, DTFT and DFT: FFT is an efficient and fast algorithm of DFT, DFT is the discrete Fourier transform of finite length sequence, DTFT is the Fourier transform of aperiodic sequence, DFT transforms the time-domain sampling of signal into the frequency-domain sampling of DTFT.

Different properties: except for the special function that the frequency of DTFT transformed graph is generally continuous and so on, its transformed graph is impact string), while DFT is equal interval sampling of DTFT, which is discrete point.

Fast Fourier transform (FFT) is actually a fast algorithm for discrete Fourier transform (DFT). Its emergence solves the problem of large amount of calculation and impracticality of DFT, and reduces the calculation of DFT by one or several orders of magnitude, so that DFT is widely used.

Different purposes: DFT is completely based on the development of computer technology, because if there is no computer, DTFT analysis can be used to see the frequency response. In order to adapt to computer calculation, it is necessary to use discrete values, because the computer can not handle continuous values, FFT is to improve the speed.
