This Website Will Help You to Learn DSP

The course of "digital signal processing" introduces that we can change the movement of things into a series of numbers, and extract useful information from them by calculation, so as to meet the needs of our practical application.

So in this website, we have four main parts to help you to learn DSP. These four parts are learning, practice, assessment and other tool. You can learn DSP by videos and text and many other ways in the part learning. Then practice in the part practice. Also, there are many ways to check your learning result in the part assessment. And in the part other tool, there are some tools helping you to learn this course.
